NEW BMW XM.

Exclusive, expressive, electrified: the new BMW XM combines expressive design with the high performance of BMW M and powerful plug-in hybrid technology of the latest generation.

  • Unsurpassed electrified M Power drive with 653 hp. provide an electric propulsion system and a new V8 engine
  • Expressive design: exclusive brand language of forms
  • Luxurious interior: M Lounge rear seat, embossed ceiling with reflected light and premium materials such as Vintage leather

Models

Electrified power.

Experience the first-ever electrified BMW M model. The M Hybrid drivetrain pairs a high-performance electric motor with a V-8 engine, generating impressive torque and up to ~30 miles of all-electric range.

Precision focused.

BMW M is renowned for exceptional engineering – and this high-performance SUV pulls out all the stops with standard Adaptive M Suspension Professional, an M Sport Differential, and the intelligent all-wheel drive power of M xDrive.

PASS THROUGH THE PRISM

Luxurious design elevates your state of mind. Sumptuous. Sculptural. Serene. Just as a beam of light is refracted through glass, the luxury of XM transfigures the conventions of form and function. For the XM-minded, this means cleverly referencing the past – while pursuing all that lies ahead.
